Aura Watch Lite

Smart, Simple, Stylish.

The Aura Watch Lite is a revolutionary wearable device designed to simplify your life while keeping you connected and healthy. It seamlessly blends essential smart features with an elegant design. Track your activity, monitor key health stats, and receive notifications, all with ease and style. Crafted with durable materials and featuring a bright display, the Aura Watch Lite offers great value and sophistication. Experience smart technology made accessible.

Key Features

  • Essential Health Monitoring: Track your heart rate, sleep patterns, and stress levels to stay informed about your well-being.
  • Smart Connectivity: Receive call, text, and app notifications directly on your wrist. Control music playback conveniently.
  • Long-lasting Battery: Enjoy extended usage with up to 7 days of battery life on a single charge, perfect for a busy lifestyle.
  • Water Resistance (IP68): Designed to withstand splashes, rain, and sweat during workouts. Suitable for everyday wear.
  • Customizable Design: Choose from various watch faces and swap interchangeable bands (sold separately) to personalize your look.
  • All-Day Fitness Tracking: Monitor your steps, distance covered, calories burned, and multiple common workout modes.
  • Intuitive Interface: Navigate features effortlessly using the responsive touchscreen display and simple controls.
  • Smart Notifications: Stay updated without reaching for your phone with alerts for calls, texts, emails, and favorite apps.
  • Connected GPS: Utilize your phone's GPS via the Aura app to accurately track pace and distance during outdoor activities.
  • Voice Assistant Compatibility: Access your phone's voice assistant (like Siri or Google Assistant) directly from the watch.

Compatibility & Charging

The Aura Watch Lite is compatible with most iOS (12.0+) and Android (7.0+) smartphones. It includes a convenient magnetic charging cable for easy power-ups. Use the free Aura app to view detailed health and fitness data, track progress, customize settings, and receive software updates that keep your watch performing its best with the latest features.
