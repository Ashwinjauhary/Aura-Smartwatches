Key Fitness Features

  • Advanced Health Monitoring: Track heart rate, sleep patterns, blood oxygen (SpO2), and stress levels accurately to optimize training and recovery.
  • Precision Fitness Tracking: Monitor steps, distance, calories, and diverse workout modes (running, cycling, swimming, yoga, HIIT, etc.).
  • Built-in GPS: Accurately track outdoor runs, rides, and hikes with integrated GPS for precise distance, pace, and route mapping.
  • 5 ATM Water Resistance: Swim-proof design allows tracking laps in the pool and wearing it worry-free in the rain or shower.
  • Personalized Workout Plans: Create and follow custom workout plans tailored to your fitness level and specific goals via the Aura app.
  • Progress Analysis: Visualize your fitness progress with detailed charts, trends, and analysis within the companion Aura app.
  • Heart Rate Zone Training: Optimize workout intensity by monitoring real-time heart rate zones (Warm-up, Fat Burn, Cardio, Peak).
  • Activity Reminders: Combat sedentary time with configurable reminders to move, stretch, or stand throughout the day.
  • In-Depth Sleep Analysis: Understand sleep quality with light, deep, and REM stage tracking, plus get personalized tips for better rest.
  • VO2 Max Estimation: Gauge your cardiorespiratory fitness level and track improvements in aerobic capacity over time.
  • Blood Pressure Monitoring: Keep an eye on your blood pressure trends directly from your wrist. (Note: For informational purposes only, not a medical device. Availability may vary.)
  • ECG Monitoring: Record an electrocardiogram (ECG) on demand to check for heart rhythm irregularities like Atrial Fibrillation (AFib). (Note: Availability varies by region/model. Not intended to replace traditional diagnosis methods.)

Compatibility & App

The Aura Smartwatch syncs seamlessly with both iOS (13.0+) and Android (8.0+) smartphones via Bluetooth 5.0. The free Aura companion app provides detailed insights into your fitness stats, health data, and sleep patterns. Customize watch faces, manage notifications, and receive regular firmware updates that deliver the latest features and performance enhancements directly to your wrist.
